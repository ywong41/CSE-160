  • Spotlight: I implemented spotlight as a flashlight attached to the camera. When Spot On is enabled, the spotlight position is the camera eye and the direction is the camera view direction (at - eye). Also, the cone width can be changed by the Spot Cutoff slider.
  • Light Motion Radius: When Light Move On is enabled, the point light moves in a circular path in the XZ plane. This slider controls the how far the light moves from the center.
