Notes to Grader:

  • Press H or click ? to open Help.
  • Click ↻ to replay the story pages and game.
  • Click canvas to rotate camera; press ESC to cancel mouse control.
  • Save/Load: Press P to save your current world (blocks + types) to the browser’s localStorage, and L to load it back (persists after refresh on the same device/browser).

    • Suggested “wow” points:

  • Terrain map: Instead of a flat base, I created terrain height map with gradual stepped hills.
  • Distance fog with day-night shift: I implemented atmospheric distance fog + subtle day/night color shift to make the feel alive.
  • Animated collectibles: Diamonds have spin + up/down motion to feel more game-like.
  • Brush-based block placement (multiple textures): I added a brush selector (keyboard 1/2/3) so players can use different textures (stone / wood / purple diamond) to build blocks in the world.

    • Hope you enjoy!
