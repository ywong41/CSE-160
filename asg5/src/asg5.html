Notes to Grader:
  • Use mouse orbit controls to navigate.
  • Click diamonds to collect them and unlock the temple.

Suggested “wow” points:

  • Diamond hunt mini game: Players can click to collect 8 diamonds placed around the world in order to unlock the temple.
  • Particle and visual effects: Collecting a diamond triggers a burst effect, and the braziers continuously emit animated ember particles.
  • Interactive audio: Sound effects play when diamonds are collected, when the temple door unlocks, and when the cat is clicked.
  • Temple unlock sequence: After all diamonds are collected, the temple door opens, the chest lid opens, and the treasure begins to glow.
  • Victory scene: The project includes a victory camera movement and spotlight sequence to highlight the victory scene.

Hope you enjoy!
